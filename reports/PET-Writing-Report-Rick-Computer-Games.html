一、学生原文 & 批注（图片式修改样式）

左侧是带红色下划线标注问题的原文，右侧是对应简要批注，版式接近“左文右批注”的阅读体验。

左侧：带标注的学生原文（红色下划线 = 需要修改）

Nowadays, computer games has1 played an important role in teenagers' free time. First of all, my friends and I are absolutely interested in playing computer games. One of the reasons is that we can get a sense of pleasure and forget the difficulties and troubles of learning during playing2 computer games. Apart from that, it also let us3 communicate more with each other and get to know each other better, which built a stronger friendship4 among us. What's more, on the optimistic side5, playing computer games not only makes us enjoy ourselves, but also exercises our minds. For instance, as we play the games, we have to think carefully about how to win them and what problems will we meet6, which helps us stay calm and find a way out while facing difficulties in real lives7. However, if we cannot control the time of playing8 computer games, we might get caught in them9 and escape from reality, which may influence our studies10. To sum up, it is okay to play computer games reasonably, instead of getting lost in the virtual world. We are supposed to make a balance11 between playing computer games and study11.

右侧：对应批注

#批注
1computer games has → computer games play：主语复数，动词用复数形式。
2during playing → while playing：表示“在…时候”更自然。
3it also let us → it also lets us：一般现在时第三人称单数加 -s。
4which built… → which builds…：此处描述一般事实，用一般现在时。
5optimistic/pessimistic side 改为 positive/negative side 更贴合议论文表达。
6what problems will we meet 改为陈述语序：what problems we might meet。
7in real lives → in real life：该语境通常用单数。
8time of playing 不自然，改为 time we spend playing。
9get caught in 可懂但不够准，沉迷建议用 get addicted to。
10influence our studies 可用，但 affect our studies 更常见。
11make a balance / and study 改为 strike a balance / and our studies（固定搭配+名词形式）。

说明：详细“原句-错误类型-修正结果”可在课堂讲评时口头展开。

二、优化范文（基于原文，高分 Band 5）

范文尽量保留学生原有结构和观点，只在语言和表达上升级。红色部分表示改写或强化。

Computer Games Nowadays, computer games play an important role in teenagers' free time. First of all, my friends and I are absolutely interested in playing them. One of the reasons is that we can get a sense of pleasure and forget the difficulties and troubles of learning while we are playing. Apart from that, computer games also allow us to communicate more with each other and get to know each other better, which builds stronger friendships among us. On the positive side, playing computer games not only helps us relax, but also exercises our minds. For instance, as we play, we have to think carefully about how to win and what problems we might meet, which helps us stay calm and find a way out when we face difficulties in real life. On the negative side, however, if we cannot control the time we spend playing computer games, we might get addicted to them and try to escape from reality, which can seriously affect our studies. To sum up, it is okay to play computer games in a reasonable way instead of getting lost in the virtual world. We are supposed to strike a balance between playing computer games and focusing on our studies.
